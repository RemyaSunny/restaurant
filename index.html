About Us

Delicious Eats has been a staple in our community since its inception in 1988 by a local Toronto-based family. In March of 1989, our doors swung open for the first time, with the entire family pouring their hearts and souls into creating a warm, inviting atmosphere. Our vision was simple: a cozy spot where great conversations flourished alongside freshly cooked meals and a well-curated selection of draft beers and spirits. Decades of dedication, innovation, and sheer determination have shaped us into what we are today. With 13 bustling locations across Toronto, we now proudly serve over 40,000 patrons every week.

Today, Delicious Eats is synonymous with mainstream casual dining, complemented by a vibrant sports bar area catering to our beloved community and local businesses. We take pride in offering friendly, attentive service within a relaxed ambiance, with every dish prepared fresh on-site.

Our menu has evolved over the years to meet the ever-changing tastes of our customers. From humble beginnings as a beer and wing joint, we now boast a diverse selection of over 50 items. Indulge in our hand-dipped, authentic beer-battered fish & chips, savor our premium brisket burgers, or opt for made-to-order Chicken tenders. Fresh-cut Ontario potatoes elevate our fries, while our extensive range includes over 20 vegetarian options. And yes, we still serve up the best wings in town, alongside our homemade salsa crafted right here on-site.

In addition to our dine-in experience, we offer convenient delivery options through our own delivery service and select third-party apps. While we value the convenience they offer, we're always mindful of the challenges they present.

The ambiance at Delicious Eats has also evolved, transitioning from a classic roadhouse vibe to a contemporary, casual dining experience. Our latest locations feature plush banquettes, warm wood finishes, and exposed brick, all accented by our signature nostalgic decor. High-definition flat-screen TVs and a curated soundtrack add to the ambiance, while our newest location even boasts its own liquor store for added convenience.

At Delicious Eats, we're not just a restaurant; we're a reflection of our community's needs and desires, continually evolving to exceed expectations

Our Menu

Pizzas

Indulge in a slice of perfection with our handcrafted pizza. Made with the finest ingredients, our signature crust is perfectly crispy on the outside and delightfully chewy on the inside. Topped with a generous layer of tangy tomato sauce, premium mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings, each bite is a burst of flavor that will leave you craving more.

MeatBalls

Savor the hearty goodness of our classic meatball dish. Hand-rolled from a blend of seasoned ground meats and breadcrumbs, our meatballs are cooked to perfection and simmered in a rich, savory marinara sauce. Each tender bite is packed with savory flavor and nostalgia, making it a comforting favorite for meat lovers.

Burgers

Sink your teeth into burger bliss with our mouthwatering selection of premium burgers. Crafted from juicy, flame-grilled beef patties, nestled between soft, toasted buns, our burgers are a true taste sensation. Customize your creation with an array of fresh toppings and savory sauces, and experience burger perfection with every bite.
